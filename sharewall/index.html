Sharewall

Dec 15th, 2013

ShareWall is a small experiment in community sharing (and building real-time apps using Meteor).

My favorite coffee shop, Tougo Coffee, installed a large HD flat panel screen. Displayed is a rotating collection of community uploaded images. Coffee drinkers can visit a mobile-friendly page to post images or messages to the screen.
